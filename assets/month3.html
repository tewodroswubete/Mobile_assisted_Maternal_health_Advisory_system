Pregnancy - Month 3

Physical Changes

Expect physical changes like weight gain, breast enlargement, and a growing baby bump. Your healthcare provider can guide you through these changes.

Antenatal Testing

Be prepared for antenatal tests, such as blood tests and ultrasounds, to monitor the baby's development and identify any potential complications.

Diet and Nutrition

Continue following a healthy diet, focusing on nutrient-rich foods and staying hydrated. Consult your healthcare provider or a nutritionist for personalized advice.

Emotional Well-being

Address any emotional challenges by seeking support from loved ones, participating in relaxation techniques, and considering prenatal classes or counseling.
