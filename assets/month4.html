Pregnancy - Month 4

Baby's Development

During this month, your baby's organs, muscles, and nervous system continue to develop. Their gender may also be determined during an ultrasound.

Physical Changes

You may experience weight gain, a noticeable baby bump, and increased breast size. Your healthcare provider can guide you through these changes.

Healthy Lifestyle

Maintain a healthy lifestyle with regular exercise, nutritious meals, and sufficient rest. Avoid smoking, alcohol, and exposure to harmful substances.

Antenatal Classes

Consider enrolling in antenatal classes to learn about labor, childbirth, and newborn care. These classes can help you prepare for the arrival of your baby.
