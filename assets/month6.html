Pregnancy - Month 6

Baby's Growth

Your baby is growing rapidly, and you may notice increased movements. Your healthcare provider may recommend a fetal ultrasound to check on their development.

Weight Gain

As your baby grows, you will continue to gain weight. It's important to maintain a healthy weight gain and discuss any concerns with your healthcare provider.

Braxton Hicks Contractions

You may start experiencing Braxton Hicks contractions, which are practice contractions that help prepare your body for labor. They are usually irregular and painless.

Sleep Disturbances

Sleep disturbances, such as insomnia and frequent bathroom visits, are common during pregnancy. Establish a bedtime routine and practice relaxation techniques.
