Pregnancy - Month 7

Third Trimester

You've entered the third trimester of pregnancy. Your baby continues to grow, and their organs are maturing in preparation for birth.

Braxton Hicks Contractions

Braxton Hicks contractions may become more frequent and intense. Monitor their pattern, as regular contractions could be a sign of preterm labor.

Shortness of Breath

As your baby grows, you may experience shortness of breath due to the pressure on your diaphragm. Take breaks, practice good posture, and use supportive pillows.

Nesting Instinct

You may feel an urge to nest, organizing and preparing your home for the arrival of your baby. Listen to your body and pace yourself while nesting.
