Pregnancy - Month 8

Baby's Weight Gain

Your baby continues to gain weight rapidly during the eighth month. They are storing fat, and their body is preparing for life outside the womb.

Pelvic Discomfort

As your baby descends into the pelvis, you may experience increased pelvic pressure and discomfort. Practice pelvic exercises and find comfortable positions.

Braxton Hicks Contractions

Braxton Hicks contractions may continue, becoming more frequent and intense. Practice relaxation techniques and monitor their pattern.

Preparing for Labor

Start preparing for labor by creating a birth plan, packing your hospital bag, and discussing pain management options with your healthcare provider.
