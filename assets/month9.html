Pregnancy - Month 9

Baby's Development

Your baby is fully developed and ready for birth. They are in the head-down position, preparing for their journey through the birth canal.

Pelvic Pressure

You may experience increased pelvic pressure and discomfort as your baby continues to descend further into the pelvis.

False Labor

Braxton Hicks contractions may become more frequent and intense, sometimes resembling true labor contractions. Time them to distinguish between false and true labor.

Final Preparations

Make final preparations for the arrival of your baby, including packing essentials for the hospital, reviewing your birth plan, and discussing any concerns with your healthcare provider.
